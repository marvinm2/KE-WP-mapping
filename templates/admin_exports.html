Regenerate Exports rebuilds the per-confidence GMT and Turtle files served under /exports/... on the live site. Use after a batch of new approvals.

Publish to Zenodo mints a new versioned deposit under the existing concept DOI. The deposit contains three per-resource ZIPs (KE-WikiPathways, KE-GO, KE-Reactome) — each with GMT files split by confidence level plus a Turtle provenance export — and a README. The deposit reflects the current live counts shown above. Use sparingly; each deposit is permanent on Zenodo.
